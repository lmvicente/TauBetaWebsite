Tau Beta Sigma - Eta Omega
For Greater Bands
The Eta Omega chapter, chartered on April 30, 1988, is located at California State University, Fresno, home of the Bulldogs.
Our members participate in various ensembles provided at Fresno State such as Fresno State's Bulldog Marching Band and Color Guard, also known as the "Best Band in the West", the scholarship group known as The Bulldog Beat, and Red Wave Indoor Percussion to name a few. Along with musicianship, we provide various service projects to the ensembles. Whether it's repairing and organizing stands in the music department during our Stand Round-Ups, repainting gack marks on the marching band's practice field, providing a reception after wind ensemble concerts, or anything else, we push to be the first to volunteer for service!
